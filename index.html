    About Acabes


    Acabes International Pvt Ltd (AIPL) provides Technology support and services to business across Arab Bank and its Affiliates and subsidiaries globally. 
    
                    AIPL is a global capability centre, supporting Arab Bank businesses worldwide, delivering innovative end-to-end business solutions in Technology and Functions. 
     Working collaboratively in the Fintech environment, AIPL is underpinned by technology Innovation and operational excellence, to ensure superlative outcomes for our customers by adopting and building innovative solution platforms.
                    
                    
     This global capability centre will provide best-in-class Technology services and together we stand committed to Arab Bank’s purpose of providing value for our customers, shareholders, business partners and the communities we serve.
